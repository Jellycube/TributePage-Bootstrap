Markus Persson

The man who changed gamedev

Markus Persson, 2014

He is the creator of this games:

  • Minecraft - his most successful game so far
  • MEG4kMAN - MegaMan clone, 4kB game
  • Left 4k Dead - zombie shooter based on Left4Dead
  • t4kns - realtime strategy
  • Miners4k - Lemmings clone
  • Dungeon4k - dungeon crawler
  • Sonic Racer 4k - racing game

"I think the only way I could make something fun and big is if I don't expect it to be."

Markus Persson
